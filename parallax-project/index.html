amazing shimla

Shimla revolves around the heart of the town - "The Mall Road". The amazing Green Valley is a breathtaking and attractive mountain range that falls on the way to Kufri from Shimla. Places to visit in Shimla "The Mall Road" , "The Ridge" , "Kufri" , "Visceral Lodge".

adventurous manali

Lush green Valleys, snowcapped mountains, breathtaking views and forests of deodar and pine, Manali is a incredible hill station at the northern end of Kullu valley in Himachal Pradesh State. Places to visit in Manali "Solang Valley" , "Hidimba Temple" , "Rohtang Pass" , "Manali Mall Road".

spiritual rishikesh

"Yoga Capital of the World". Rishikesh is also a pilgrimage town and one of the holiest places for Hindus. It lies on the holy banks of river Ganga and Gateway to Garhwal Himalayas. Places to visit in Rishikesh "The Beatles Ashram" , "Lakshman Jhula" , "Triveni Ghat" , "Neelkantha Mahadeva Temple".

bhimtal : Lake City

Bhimtal is a easy on the eye hill station, the charm of which lies in its off-beat, tranquil atmosphere. Sattal Lake is another lake nearby that tourists can visit.Places to visit in Bhimtal "Sattal" , "Hidimba Parvat" , "Hanuman Garhi"

Kasol

Kasol is a little village in Himachal situated along the banks of the river Parvati. Kasol is also known as 'Amsterdam of India'. Places to visit in Kasol "Parvati River" , "Tosh" , "Manikaran Sahib"
